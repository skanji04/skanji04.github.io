Visualizing the Goodreads Choice Awards




Whenever I’m looking for a new book, I’ll often head towards the Goodreads Choice Awards, click one of the winners and go from there. Yes, the process is not one hundred percent perfect since Goodreads chooses the nominations, but it’s a decent place to start. Generally, books that win are quality reads. While I was getting lost in the folds of the Goodreads website after clicking on the 2020 autobiography winner, A Promised Land by Barack Obama, I wondered if books in specific genres had similar colors in their title covers. It seemed like a fun thing to visualize, so I collected the winners of 5 genres for the past 10-12 years based on when the category was introduced. Below are the most prominent colors from each title cover taken from Goodreads. If you click on the color palette, it will take you to its specific Goodreads page.
